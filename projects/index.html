Ziri

Ziri

Ziri is a Slack app that helps one retweet a tweet from Slack by adding a reaction to the message that contains a tweet link.

The app has powered over 18,807 retweets and tweet favorites for over 817 users across more than 108 Slack teams thereby saving users thousands of minutes of clicking tweet links and going to Twitter to retweet/fav a tweet.

MakeCV

MakeCV

MakeCV is a resume builder that collects user's raw information in an easy-to-fill form and produces a beautiful resume with a very customizable layout.

With MakeCV you can create different resumes with different focus information and different style and layout based on the company's and job role's needs. You can make different CVs for different job applications.
